Good to Know

Questions we hear the most

  • What are the dinner hours?

    Spaulding Steak & Ale serves dinner Tuesday through Saturday from 4 PM, opening with happy hour. Sunday brunch runs 8 AM to 2 PM. Call (603) 332-0107 to hold a table.

  • Can I stay overnight?

    Yes - twenty guest rooms, every one original, all with private bath, A/C, cable, and Wi-Fi, with breakfast included. Book online or call the front desk.

  • Is there live music, and does it cost anything?

    Five nights a week. Acoustic nights at Spaulding (Wednesday and Thursday, 6–9 PM) are free. Weekend bands at The Garage (Friday & Saturday 7–10 PM, Sunday concerts 5–8 PM, May–October) are usually a $7 cover, $10 for select shows.

  • Can I order takeout?

    Yes - order via Toast every night the restaurant is open.

  • Do you host weddings and private events?

    Yes - ceremonies at the gazebo, receptions under the tented courtyard or in the ballroom, plus showers, rehearsal dinners, celebrations of life, and business events. Call (603) 332-0107 for the events team.

  • Where are you?

    78 Wakefield Street, Rochester, NH 03867 - the historic Spaulding estate. Look for the hanging Restaurant & Hotel sign out front.
